Testimonials

Weston

I went on my first mission trip in October 2018 to Belize with RCMI. It was one of the most eye opening moments I've ever had. God moves in so many different ways, and I realized how under privileged so many other countries are. People like you and me can make a difference no matter how big or small! He took this small town country boy across the seas and I had the time of my life fellowshipping with some amazing people of Christ.



Tiffany

Going to Haiti in 2013 was a life changing experience. This trip came at a time in my life when I needed a push in the right direction. I will never forget how the people of Haiti truly appreciated God's will. It is nothing like we see in America today. There's many ways to witness how God works through others; but a short-term missions trip is where it's at.



Monte & Amy

Going to Guatemala has opened up my heart to change! Changed in the way I pray, in the way I fellowship with others, in the way I worship, in the way I give and the way I love. I go to Guatemala to be the hands and feet for Jesus and I always come back, and with new friends.



Pastor Drew Brassfield

Church of the Hills in Lometa, Texas. I was raised in church around missionaries and dreamed of going on mission trips some day. One Wednesday night in 2018 God spoke to me and said go to Belize with Dr. Paul Olson. The trip was life changing. We got to teach precious people of God, pray over the sick, and be a blessing to a wonderful church in a small village in Belize. The last night of the trip I was laying in bed and had an amazing encounter with The Lord that's hard to describe. The Lord ministered to me deeply. I will never see missions the same again. Every believer should go on a mission trip at least once in their life. It’s life changing. I’m thankful for the ministry of RCMI and Dr. Paul & Marcy Olson. They are the real deal missionaries. I can’t wait until the next opportunity to GO and be the hands and feet of Jesus.
