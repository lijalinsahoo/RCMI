Mission Trips

Refuge CMI leads mission trips to various countries in Central America and the Caribbean Nations. The purpose of each mission trip varies as the list and pictures below show. Most of our trips are open to anyone who has a Christian testimony to share. However, occasionally we embark on trips that require certain skills of the participants, such as training experience, age restrictions, etc.
