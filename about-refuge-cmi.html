About Refuge CMI

“And in His Name will be the HOPE of all the world.” Matthew 12:21 NLT

Welcome to Refuge Christian Ministries International

Refuge Christian Ministries International’s theme is “Training Pastors & Leaders…Giving Hope to the Hopeless!” In the first 10 years of our ministry, our focus was on children and children still are the heart of our ministry. We also minister in many other ways, as you will see as you travel through this website. Refuge CMI accomplishes this great task in three ways:

ENCOURAGE :
Sharing the Good News of the Gospel at every opportunity,
Providing outreach (medical, VBS, crusades, etc) to the needy,

EMPOWER :
Training national workers to teach, preach, and help their people more effectively,
Provide resources and tools to the national churches (ministry tools, construction supplies, etc),
Provide tools for vocational businesses (sewing machines, etc),

ENGAGE :
Take US short-term teams to the mission field,
Take church members to their villages to do outreach and evangelism.

This is done with direct hands on ministry and through partnerships with other ministries and organizations; both here and around the world. All of this happens through the prayers and financial support of those who believe in what we are doing for the Kingdom of God. Thank you for your support!
