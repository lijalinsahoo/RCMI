Refuge CMI

RCMI School

USA Training:

Refuge CMI provides mission trip preparation training for those individuals in the USA who wish to travel with Refuge CMI on a mission trip for the first time. It covers many aspects of doing mission work in another country and another culture. It also helps the potential short-term missionary get a handle on their role in sharing the gospel in a different environment. A certificate of completion is presented to the participant upon completion of the course. Contact Refuge CMI to register.

Foreign Field:

Refuge CMI offers training to national pastors and leaders in specific subjects to help them be more effective in their respective ministries. This training is offered on the mission field in a seminar setting and certificates of completion are presented to the participants.

Online Training:

Refuge CMI has partnered with Vision Online Learning to offer a full line of study to prepare the participant for ministry as follows:
Online Course for Christian Ministry Training

Refuge CMI offers an online RCMI School of Ministry program in partnership with Vision, that enables anyone, anywhere, to earn various Certificates of Ministry online through our website. Choose from dozens of courses in Christian Ministry. Enroll in one course at a time with affordable, 0% interest monthly financing. Vision maintains a transcript of courses taken and present certificates of work completed. This is a great opportunity to be better equipped for more effective ministry wherever you are located.
