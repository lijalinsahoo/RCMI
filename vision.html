Vision & Faith

VISION

Every child in the world hears the Good News of the Gospel while their hearts are tender and they are in the 4-14 Window. Indigenous pastors and leaders in the nations we are called to minister in receive training to be more effective in shepherding their congregations and reaching their nations.

Churches in America catch the vision to “Go…” (the Great Commission) into all the world starting with their communities. There are many churches who do nothing to reach their own communities and cities, let alone having a vision for the world. We want to see churches have a vision for going and doing all the way from their children’s department, through their youth department, and all the way up through the adults. The Great Commission should be in the very fabric of every church’s culture, because the Great Commission was NOT the Great Omission!

WE BELIEVE…

In one God, eternally existent in three persons: Father, Son & Holy Spirit. The Bible is the divinely inspired and infallible Word of God. In the deity of Jesus Christ: His virgin birth, His sinless life, His death and resurrection, His ascension to Heaven.

Man was created in the image & likeness of God, but fell through disobedience, and is born with a sinful nature. Salvation has been provided through Christ alone for all mankind. Through Christ’s redemptive work on the cross, we have healing in answer to prayer.

Water baptism by immersion is a testimony of the believer’s new life. In observing Holy Communion, remembering the sacrifice of Jesus Christ on the cross.

The Holy Spirit is a divine person sent to indwell, guide, teach, and empower the believer. It is the will of God that each believer be filled with the Holy Spirit, receiving power for holy living (a holy life separated from sin & the world) and effective service.

The Church consists of all those who believe on the Lord Jesus Christ, redeemed through His blood, and are born again of the Spirit. In the second coming of Christ; the rapture of the Church; the bodily resurrection of the saved to eternal reward, the lost to eternal judgment.
