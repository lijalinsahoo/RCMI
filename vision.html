Our Vision

Our Vision is that…

Every child in the world hears the Good News of the Gospel while their hearts are tender.

Indigenous pastors and leaders in the nations we minister in receive training to be more effective in shepherding their congregations and reaching their nations.

Churches in America catch the vision to “Go…” (the Great Commission) into all the world starting with their communities. There are many churches who do nothing to reach their own communities and cities, let alone having a vision for the world. We want to see churches have a vision for “going and doing” all the way from their children’s department, through their youth department, and all the way up through the adults. The Great Commission should be in the very fabric of every church’s culture, because the Great Commission was NOT the Great Suggestion, it is a command directly from Christ Himself before He went to Heaven!
