Our Partners

PRAYER PARTNERS

We need you to partner with us in prayer. We are setting up a Partners In Prayer Network and would love for you to be a part of this very important network. As needs arise here on the Home Missions Field or on the Foreign Mission Field, we will text you and/or email you with the prayer request, which in most cases you will also be encouraged to pass the need on to other prayer warriors you know in your church or family. To be a part of this vital partnership ministry, we will need your phone number for texts and your email for emailed requests. You can provide this information in our CONTACT US page here on this website.


FINANCIAL PARTNERS

As the old saying goes, “It takes money to make the world go around!” (not necessarily theologically sound , it also takes money to accomplish getting the gospel out to the nations. Refuge CMI is no exception, and we so appreciate all our financial partners. We have several ways you can support Refuge CMI financially. First, there is the good old fashioned way of writing a check to Refuge CMI and sending it to: Refuge CMI – PO Box 58 – Goldthwaite, TX. 76844-0058 Second, you can go to the DONATE buttons on this website and make a secure donation by credit/debit card through the PayPal platform. Third, you can download our RCMI phone app and be able to access the DONATE buttons on our website that way. For more information on how to do any of these methods, give us a call or shoot us an email, and we will help you get it done. Thanks, and God bless you!
