Our Work

Refuge Christian Ministries International works diligently to meet the ever-changing needs of the church in America and around the world. Refuge CMI provides increased support and training for their partner ministries.

TRAINING :

• Church Leadership Workshop
• Spiritual Gifts Inventory
• Uniquely You Workshops (combine D.I.S.C. profiles with spiritual gifts)
• Mission Preparation Workshops
• Witnessing Effectively to Cults

SHORT-TERM MISSION TRIPS :

Haiti – Domincan Republic – Belize – Guatemala – Jamaica – Puerto Rico – other areas as the Lord leads

TYPES OF MISSION ACTIVITIES :

• Medical Outreaches
• Life Skills Training
• Pastors/Workers Training
• Outreach, Evangelism, VBS
• Construction
• Feeding Programs (Hot Meals, Food Parcels)
• Water Programs
• Clothing Distribution
• Ministry Support (Bicycles, Bibles, Literature, Training)
• Sponsorships (Orphans, Widows, Indigenous Pastors/Evangelists)

PULPIT FILL-IN :

Challenging messages to encourage and motivate the Body of Christ to move out of the four walls of the church and reach their neighbors and communities with the HOPE and the Gospel message, and meeting needs in their communities and around the world.
